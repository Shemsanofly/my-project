Top Zanzibar Destination

Experience paradise on Earth - discover the best places to visit in Zanzibar.

Prison Island

Home to giant tortoises and historical ruins just off the coast of Stone Town.

Paje Beach

A favorite for kite-surfing and beach lovers with beautiful sunrise views.

Kendwa Rocks

A vibrant beach destination known for parties, diving, and golden sunsets.

Mnemba Atoll

Perfect spot for snorkeling and seeing coral reefs and colorful fish.

Spice Farms

Experience the scents and flavors of Zanzibar through spice tours and tastings.

The Rock Restaurant

A world-famous seafood restaurant located on a rock in the Indian Ocean.
